Visit Skywalk
by electric bikes

Nature Park Biokovo is now home of the most attractive walkway in Croatia! Biokovo Skywalk is located at Ravna Vlaška viewpoint, 1228 meters above sea level. It is a walking surface at the edge of a cliff that enables visitors to enjoy extreme and unforgettable walks in controlled conditions. We are here to make it possible for you to reach this beautiful destination using our high-quality electric bikes!

Rent an e-bike!

We offer high-quality electric bikes of Haibike and Cube brand that are equipped with strong batteries of 400, 500 and 625 Wh which is enough not only to reach Skywalk, but also the very top of Biokovo - Sv. Jure. And of course, we offer regular bikes as well. Also, feel free to ask for a helmet, lock, maps, child seat or even a repairing kit, it is all included in price.

Why e-bikes?

It’s no secret that cars usually wait a few hours at the entrance of Biokovo nature park. However, bikes have priority and for them there is no waiting! If you take this trip by an e-bike you will be able to adjust your own level of assistance. Not only is it a healthy activity, but also breathtaking adventure you’ll always remember.
