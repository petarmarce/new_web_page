Who are we?

Our Team is a passionate group of five family members and our company name is "Rent a Bike & E-Bike Momentum Makarska". We have been in bike renting business for 12 years already, so we know what our customers want! :)

What do we offer?

We offer high quality electric bikes of Haibike and Cube brand that are equipped with strong batteries of 400 and 500 Wh which is enough not only to reach Skywalk, but also the very top of Biokovo - Sv. Jure. And of course, we offer regular bikes as well. Also, feel free to ask for a helmet, lock, maps, child seat or even a repairing kit, it is all included in price.

Why E-Bikes?

It is not a secret that cars usually wait a few hours at the entrance of Biokovo nature park. However, the bikes have priority and for them there is no waiting. If you take this trip by an e-bike you will be able to adjust your own level of assistance. Also it is healthy and it is an adventure.
