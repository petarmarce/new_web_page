Skywalk
photo gallery

Nature Park Biokovo is now home of the most attractive walkway in Croatia! Biokovo Skywalk is located at Ravna Vlaška viewpoint, 1228 meters above sea level. It is a walking surface at the edge of a cliff that enables visitors to enjoy extreme and unforgettable walks in controlled conditions. We are here to make it possible for you to reach this beautiful destination using our high-quality electric bikes!
